Making Internet Copyright Laws Known

Creative works are taken all the time online without due credit. That's why I made Project: CC&C (Creative Credit & Copyright). This website aims to spread awareness of the internet's copyright regulations, introduce the topic of Creative Commons, and provide useful tools to help users choose between different licenses.

What is Copyright?

Copyright is a form of protection for your original work. It can be used with literature, artwork, music, videos, and anything else that's creative and yours. When you have copyright over a work, you can manage how people are allowed to use, edit, and distribute it. There are many different forms of copyright, ranging from All Rights Reserved to Creative Commons to Public Domain. You can find some of the symbols commonly associated with copyright on the right.

Copyright is society's shield against property theft. Knowing how to use copyright can allow you to establish ownership of your own works AND know when it is appropiate to modify the creative works of others.
